ASCS MUN'26

I'm proud to have served as Chief of Staff of ASCS MUN 2026, my high school's annual Model United Nations Conference. This was a 3-day conference on Feb 6-8 2026, with 470 students attending from across the UAE and a ~22k budget.

As Chief of Staff, I was responsible for handling/ scheduling 200 interviews, and personally conducting 90 of them. I then trained a team of 62 over 3 months in preparation for the conference.

I've had a loooong journey attending MUNs over the years, and I absolutely love them. Being part of ASCS MUN's Secretariat was truly a dream come true, and the conference itself was phenomenal. (shoutout to the other members of the secretariat, you guys were the best)
