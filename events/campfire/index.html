Campfire Dubai

In February 2026, I ran Campfire Dubai. This was a game jam 100% by teens, for teens taking place at the Microsoft MEA Headquarters in Dubai. Campfire also ran in another 200+ cities worldwide!

I spent two months working on Campfire Dubai. If you're interested to know more, you can check out this journal entry.
